Biography

XXXTentacion

Who was XXXTentacion?
Jahseh Dwayne Ricardo Onfroy (Plantation, January 23, 1998 – Deerfield Beach, June 18, 2018), also known as XXXTentacion, was an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He is regarded by many as one of the most influential artists of his generation, blending genres such as hip-hop, emo, and punk rock. Much of XXXTentacion's work is noted for its raw and emotional exploration of themes like mental health, depression, and social issues, making him a symbol of openness and vulnerability in modern music. Despite his brief career, his impact on the music industry and his dedicated fanbase remains profound.

XXXTentacion was born in Plantation, Florida, and spent much of his early life in Lauderhill. In 2013, he began his musical career, initially uploading songs to the online audio distribution platform SoundCloud. His breakthrough came in 2017 with the single "Look at Me," which brought him national attention for its aggressive style and raw energy. XXXTentacion's debut album, "17" (2017), explored themes of depression and mental health, earning both commercial success and critical acclaim. He continued to build his legacy with his second album, "?" (2018), which debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 and further solidified his influence in the music industry.

PERSONAL LIFE

XXXTentacion

Jahseh Dwayne Ricardo Onfroy was born on January 23, 1998, in Plantation, Florida. Although he faced a tumultuous childhood, he found solace in music at an early age. Onfroy was primarily raised by his grandmother due to his mother’s financial struggles. His music often reflected his challenging upbringing, with themes of pain, trauma, and emotional turmoil. XXXTentacion had two siblings, an older half-sister named Ariana and a younger half-brother named Aiden. Despite his short life, XXXTentacion's impact on music and culture was profound, resonating deeply with fans who connected with his raw and unfiltered expression of personal struggles.

The Friendship of Ski Mask the Slump God and XXXTentacion
XXXTentacion met rapper Ski Mask the Slump God while they were both in juvenile detention. Their friendship quickly grew, leading them to form the collective Members Only, which became a platform for their music and collaboration. Ski Mask had a significant presence in XXXTentacion's early career, often performing together and appearing in each other's music videos. Despite occasional public disagreements, Ski Mask has frequently expressed deep respect and admiration for XXXTentacion, describing their relationship as a brotherhood founded on mutual understanding and support. After XXXTentacion's untimely death, Ski Mask continued to honor his legacy through his music and public tributes.

XXXTentacion's Relationships
XXXTentacion's personal life was marked by several significant relationships. He dated Geneva Ayala, who became well-known due to her allegations of abuse against him, which brought significant media attention and controversy. Despite the turbulent nature of their relationship, Ayala has expressed sorrow over his death. At the time of his murder, XXXTentacion was expecting a child with his girlfriend Jenesis Sanchez. Their son, Gekyume Onfroy, was born on January 26, 2019, continuing XXXTentacion's legacy. His relationships, both romantic and platonic, often influenced his music, contributing to the emotional depth and complexity of his work.

MUSICAL CAREER

XXXTentacion

In 2014, XXXTentacion debuted as a national rapper, releasing music on SoundCloud and quickly gaining attention for his raw and aggressive style. His breakout single, "Look at Me," released in 2015, brought him widespread recognition and became a viral hit in 2017. XXXTentacion's first two albums, "17" (August 2017) and "?" (March 2018), showcased his versatility and emotional depth, earning critical acclaim and commercial success. He was also part of the collective Members Only, releasing several collaborative albums with the group, including "Members Only, Vol. 3" (June 2017). Rapper and close friend Ski Mask the Slump God participated in many of these projects, contributing significantly to their sound and success.
